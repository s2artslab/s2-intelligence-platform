# PSLA pre-filing exploration

## Purpose

Users have not filed in court. They compare administrative, state court, federal court, agency, arbitration, and negotiation paths before opening a formal matter.

## Path types

- **negotiation** — demand letters, settlement talks; often required before suit.
- **administrative** / **agency** — exhaustion of remedies (EEOC, unemployment appeals, licensing boards).
- **state_court** — state trial courts; know statute of limitations and service rules.
- **federal_court** — requires federal question or diversity; procedural posture differs from state.
- **arbitration** — if contract mandates arbitration, court may lack jurisdiction until arbitration completes.

## Compare dimensions

For each path: prerequisites, timeline, cost/complexity, risks of wrong forum, and whether filing starts a clock (SOL, administrative deadlines).

## Verification

All deadlines and rule citations must be verified against official court and agency sources.
